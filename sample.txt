Here are few lines on the uses of coding:
Foundation of Technology:
Coding is the language through which we instruct computers to perform tasks, forming the basis of all digital technologies. 
Software and App Development:
Coders build the software and applications we use daily, from operating systems to mobile apps, shaping our digital experiences. 
Automation and Efficiency:
Coding allows for the automation of repetitive tasks, boosting efficiency and productivity across various industries. 
Problem-Solving and Innovation:
Coding empowers individuals to tackle complex problems and develop innovative solutions by leveraging computational thinking. 
Career Opportunities:
Coding skills are in high demand, opening doors to diverse and lucrative career paths in the tech industry and beyond.
